Welcome to Wild Blodyn

Hi, I'm Bethann and thank you for visiting my website. I have been a florist for just over a year now and I absolutely love it!


I've always been into activities that allow me to express myself through creativity and flowers are my favourite medium. I enjoy creating bouquets to suit all occasions and source all my flowers locally, be that from local businesses or foraging.


Please browse my page for inspiration and get in touch if you'd like to place an order.
